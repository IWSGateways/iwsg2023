Tübingen and surroudings

Tübingen is a university town in the state of Baden-Württemberg, Germany. It is located on the Ammer and Ammersee rivers and is known for its well-preserved medieval old town and its prestigious University of Tübingen, which was founded in 1477. The town is also known for its picturesque streets, charming architecture, and numerous cultural and recreational activities, making it a popular tourist destination. Tübingen is also home to several research institutions and companies, making it a center for innovation and technology. An ideal place to live, have fun, practice sport.

Tübingen will surprise you: a small, ever evolving city that remains deeply attached to its traditions.
