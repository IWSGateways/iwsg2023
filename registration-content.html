Accommodation

Tübingen is a popular town with sometimes very limited availability of hotel rooms. Therefore finalize your travel plans as soon as possible and book a room!

We were able to organize a limited contingent of hotel rooms in Tübingen. The Hotel Domizil is located directly beside the Neckar river less than 5 minutes walking distance from the conference venue. The apartments offered through Cloud No. 7 are similarly just around the corner.

For booking please contact them through info@hotel-domizil.de or operations@studios.cloudno7.de and refer to IWSG2023. Please directly provide the usual base information like name, length of your stay, room type, billing address, etc.

Please note that the contingents are only available till 10.05.2023 or 15.05.2023, respectively.
